TACIC90 Competition Competition Rules and Regulations:

The competition consists of several sections:
  • 1. Predictions of the Champions League (all matches).
  • 2 - La Liga expectations, only matches teams (Real Madrid, Barcelona, Atletico Madrid, Valencia, Sevilla, Villarreal).
  • 3 - Expectations of the Premier League, only matches teams (Manchester United, Chelsea, Liverpool, Man City, Arsenal, Tottenham).

Monthly Contest System "Champion of the Month"

  • The monthly contest is based on the Gregorian month and the timing of the contest, which is the timing of Mecca.
  • The winner of the "Champion of the Month" is the participant who collects the highest number of points each calendar month in all combined leagues (La Liga, Premier League, Serie A, and Champions League).
  • The monthly contest points are counted on a page separate from the season-long TACTIC90 competition.
  • The monthly contest starts every month on 1 and ends at the end of the month (30, 31 or 28/29)
  • If the start of the match on 31/12/2016 at 11:59 pm, the match is considered as part of the December competition regardless of the rest of the tour.
  • If the start of the match on 01/01/2017 at 00:00 is considered the match of the system in the competition in January, regardless of the rest of the tour.
  • The winner has the largest number of points and in the case of the equal number of participants or more, the usual priorities (more joker, then more accurate, then less wrong, then the highest ranking in the Champions League for the season as a whole)
  • Each month, the winner receives an award which is the preferred team shirt (depending on availability).
  • The number of matches in the monthly competition varies from month to month, according to the European football league agendas, but the number of games per month will not be less than 50 games,
  • You may combine more than one monthly prize in the season for the same subscriber.
  • The monthly and seasonal competition prize may be combined.
